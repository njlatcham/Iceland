Iceland

A landscape of legends

Land of

Ice & Snow

Yes, Robert Plant and Co. were so moved by the land of ice and snow while playing a gig in the capital Reykjavik that they decided to pay homage to the nation in verse, and wrote and fineshed the track in just six days, in time for their next show.

That Iceland is so powerful as to inform the music of one of the world's biggest rock bands, it is no surprise that it continues to enjoy unprecendented popularity, welcoming more and more visitors to its marvellous shores.
